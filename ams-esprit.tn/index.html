SDGs

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 global goals established by the United Nations to address social, economic, and environmental challenges and promote sustainable development worldwide. At AMS, we are committed to aligning our efforts with these goals to contribute to a more sustainable and equitable future.

SDG 3 - Good Health and Well-being

AMS improves healthcare management, enhances patient care, and enables efficient resource allocation, leading to better health outcomes.

SDG 4 - Quality Education

AMS supports training and development initiatives for healthcare professionals, promoting continuous learning and improved healthcare delivery.

SDG 5 - Gender Equality

AMS fosters gender equality by providing equal opportunities and resources for healthcare providers, ensuring inclusivity and fairness.

SDG 9 - Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

AMS leverages innovative technologies, such as AI, IoT, and data analytics, to drive advancements in healthcare management and infrastructure.

SDG 10 - Reduced Inequalities

AMS addresses inequalities by improving access to quality healthcare services and promoting equitable healthcare resource allocation.

SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities

AMS supports sustainable healthcare systems, promoting efficient resource utilization and integrated healthcare services in urban and rural areas.

SDG 13 - Climate Action

AMS contributes to climate action by promoting telemedicine and digital solutions, reducing carbon emissions associated with unnecessary travel for medical consultations.

SDG 17 - Partnerships for the Goals

AMS collaborates with healthcare institutions, technology providers, and stakeholders to foster partnerships and achieve shared goals in healthcare management.

Modules

At AMS, we offer a range of specialized modules designed to cater to different aspects of healthcare management. Each module is tailored to address specific needs and challenges faced by healthcare providers.

  • Patient Management
  • Employee Management
  • Communication Centers
  • Hospital Management
  • Ambulance Management

Patient Management

Managing Patients

Our Patient Management module provides comprehensive features for efficient patient registration, appointment scheduling, medical record management, and diagnostic tracking. It enables healthcare providers to streamline patient workflows, ensure accurate documentation, and enhance overall patient experience.

Employee Management

Manage Employees

The Employee Management module offers functionalities to manage healthcare staff, including authentication with features like fingerprint scanning and QR code verification. It also includes tools for employee scheduling, performance tracking, and training management, facilitating efficient workforce management.

Communication Centers

Manage Communication Centers and incomming emergency calls

The Communication Centers module integrates communication channels, such as voice messaging and real-time chat, to enable seamless collaboration and information sharing among healthcare professionals. It enhances communication efficiency, ensuring prompt response to critical situations and effective coordination among team members.

Hospital Management

Manage Hospitals and incomming emergencies

With our Hospital Management module, healthcare institutions can efficiently manage various aspects of hospital operations. This includes inventory management, resource allocation, bed management, and financial administration. It enables hospitals to optimize resources, improve cost-effectiveness, and streamline overall operations.

Ambulance Management

Manage Ambulances and incomming emergency calls

Our Ambulance Management module focuses on optimizing ambulance services. It includes features such as real-time tracking, route optimization, and automated dispatching, ensuring timely response and efficient utilization of ambulance resources. It helps healthcare providers improve emergency response times and deliver prompt medical assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find Frequently Asked Questions about our services at AMS

  • Is AMS compatible with existing healthcare systems?

    Yes, AMS is designed to integrate seamlessly with existing healthcare systems, ensuring interoperability and data exchange for efficient collaboration and streamlined workflows.

  • Can AMS be customized to meet specific requirements?

    Absolutely! AMS is a flexible solution that can be tailored to the unique needs of your organization. Our team will work closely with you to understand your requirements and customize the system accordingly.

  • How secure is AMS in terms of data protection?

    Data security is a top priority for us. AMS incorporates advanced security measures, including encryption, access controls, and data backup protocols, to safeguard sensitive information and ensure compliance with privacy regulations.

  • What type of support and training do you provide?

    We offer comprehensive support and training to ensure a smooth implementation and optimal usage of AMS. Our team provides training sessions, user manuals, and ongoing technical support to assist you at every step of the way.

  • Can AMS scale as our organization grows?

    Absolutely! AMS is designed to be scalable and adaptable to the evolving needs of your organization. Whether you expand to multiple locations or increase your patient volume, AMS can accommodate your growth and continue to deliver efficient healthcare management.
